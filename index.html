Frequently Asked Questions

How is this better?

The Plastic Roads will last longer, use recycled plastic, and take less time to put in and replace.

What about drainage?

The Plastic Road has three drainage points to ensure the roads wont flood and help keep the roads safe.

How do is it installed?

You simply drop the pieces in next to the other section of road. Future designs will have connection points.
